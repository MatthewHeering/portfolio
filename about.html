About

Mechanical design, analysis, and build work with a systems mindset.

I am a mechanical engineering student at Cornell University interested in the full path from problem framing to working hardware. My projects span CAD, finite element analysis, fluid mechanics, system dynamics, mechatronics, and technical reporting.

I like engineering work that asks for both judgment and precision: choosing the right model, understanding what the simplifications mean, and making the result clear enough to support a design decision.

Across my coursework and projects, I have become especially interested in how analysis changes design. A useful model does more than produce a number; it explains tradeoffs, exposes weak points, and points toward the next iteration.
