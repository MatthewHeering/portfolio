Interests

Technical areas I keep returning to.

I am drawn to problems where physical systems, simulation, and design decisions meet. These areas shape the projects I choose and the tools I keep building.

Mechanical design

Useful geometry

Designing parts and assemblies around constraints, load paths, usability, and manufacturability.

Simulation

Models with purpose

Using FEA and system models to answer specific design questions, not just produce plots.

Robotics

Machines that respond

Connecting mechanical design, sensing, programming, and controls into working systems.

Fluid mechanics

Flow in hardware

Understanding how pressure, motion, power, and measurement show up in real devices.

CAD

Design communication

Using models and drawings to reason about form, fit, assembly, and tradeoffs.

Engineering judgment

Clear decisions

Balancing performance, safety, simplicity, and human use when a problem is open-ended.
