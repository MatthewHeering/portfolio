Mechanical design

Torque Wrench Design Project

For Mechanics of Materials, my team designed and analyzed a torque wrench, moving from CAD geometry to material selection, FEA setup, stress and strain interpretation, and strain gauge sensitivity calculations.

The project emphasized the full analysis chain: define loads and constraints, choose an appropriate material, evaluate deformation and stress, then connect the numerical results to a measurement strategy.

Technologies used: Ansys, Fusion 360, MATLAB, FEA, strain gauge analysis
