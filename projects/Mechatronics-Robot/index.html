Mechatronics Robot Felix

In Mechatronics, my team designed, built, and programmed Felix, a robot made to collect blocks in a final competition. The project combined mechanical design, electronics, controls, sensor behavior, and fast iteration under a deadline.

On competition day, Felix went 5-2 overall and reached the quarterfinals. The result came from repeated testing and practical debugging: improving reliability mattered as much as any single design feature.

Technologies used: Arduino, Fusion 360, sensors, controls, prototyping
