Fluid Mechanical Dissection of a Rocking Piston Air Compressor

For my Fluid Mechanics class, my group dissected a rocking piston air compressor and analyzed it through the framework of the course material. We studied the system from fluid container boundary movement to measured flow rate and power.

Technologies used: Anemometer, visual tachometer
