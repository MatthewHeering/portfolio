Fluid mechanics

Rocking Piston Air Compressor Dissection

For Fluid Mechanics, my group dissected a rocking piston air compressor and translated its physical operation into course concepts. We studied the moving piston boundaries, traced the flow path, and connected measured behavior to power and flow-rate analysis.

The work emphasized engineering communication as much as teardown. The final video explained how the system functions and why the measured quantities matter for describing compressor performance.

Technologies used: Anemometer, visual tachometer, system teardown, technical video
